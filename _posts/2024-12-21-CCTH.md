## How to passed the CCTH Examination

Passing the Certified Cyber Threat Hunter (CCTH) examination requires a strong foundation in using Cybereason's platform and tools. If you're unfamiliar with Cybereason, it's essential to complete the training modules they provide. These modules are specifically designed to help you understand the platform and its features, ensuring you're ready to tackle the exam effectively.

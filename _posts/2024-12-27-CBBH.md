## How to passed the CBBH Examination

1. Finish the modules of CBBH, and even if you've completed them, do the assessment if you're unsure. 
2. Use the global search in HTB Academy while taking the exam, it can be really helpful, especially when you can't find something in your notes or if you've forgotten something. 
3. Use AI to assist with your report, and the CVSS calculator for scoring. 
4. Most importantly, ensure you have thorough information about the target and complete the reconnaissance to avoid missing anything.
5. If you are not confident you can do labs from Portswigger Academy and setup a labs from VulnHub.
6. Always take a break so that you don't burn out.
7. Black Coffee :>
